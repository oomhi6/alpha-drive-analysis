**Ginkgo Biloba** — Primarily a circulation herb. Better blood flow means better erectile function. Simple cause and effect. It also supports mental clarity, which matters more than people admit when it comes to sexual confidence.

**Muira Puama (Root)** — Called "nature's aphrodisiac" in Brazilian traditional medicine. It addresses both the physical and psychological sides of low libido — useful for men who find that stress and mental noise are the actual obstacle.

The synergy angle matters here. Each herb addresses a different pressure point — energy, circulation, hormone balance, libido, mental state. Used together, they're not redundant. They're complementary.
